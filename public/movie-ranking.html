My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Predator

Classic Sci-Fi Movie. Great Special Effects with intense story line of the battle between man and an Alien Hunter.

War Of The Worlds

Great Remake Of The H.G. Wells Classic.

Ford VS Ferrari

The plot follows a determined team of American and English engineers and designers, led by automotive designer Carroll Shelby and his English driver, Ken Miles to beat Enzo Ferrari in the Le Mans.
